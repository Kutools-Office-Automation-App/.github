## 🚀 Introduction
Kutools for Windows is a productivity add-in suite built by ExtendOffice that extends Microsoft Office with hundreds of ready-made tools. Rather than clicking through nested menus or writing macros, you install Kutools once and it adds its own ribbon tabs to Excel, Outlook, and Word, giving you a single button for each chore you would otherwise do by hand. The suite has three main products, and Kutools for Excel is the most popular by a wide margin because of how many utilities it delivers. It is a paid product with a free trial, so you can put it through its paces before deciding.

## ✨ Highlights

### Everyday automation
- **Kutools for Excel** — Hundreds of Excel utilities covering batch operations, merging and splitting workbooks, combining rows and columns, advanced find and replace, and one-click cleanup of blanks, duplicates, and inconsistent formatting.
- **One-click utilities** — Long, error-prone sequences become single ribbon buttons, so routine spreadsheet and document work moves far faster.

### Beyond the spreadsheet
- **Kutools for Outlook** — Tools for bulk email handling, one-click replies with attachments, automatic CC/BCC rules, and quicker sorting, forwarding, and inbox management.
- **Kutools for Word** — Batch document utilities that add or remove content across many files, convert tables and images, and tidy formatting throughout large documents.
- **Native ribbon integration** — Installs as an add-in with its own tabs inside each Office app, keeping every tool within reach as you work.

## 💻 System Specs

**Minimum**
- Windows 10 64-bit
- A supported version of Microsoft Office or Microsoft 365
- A modern 64-bit processor
- Enough RAM to meet your Office version's minimum
- A few hundred MB of free storage for the add-in

**Recommended**
- Windows 11 64-bit
- The latest supported Microsoft 365 release
- A recent multi-core 64-bit processor
- 8 GB of RAM or more
- 1 GB or more of free storage

## 🎯 Getting Up and Running
1. Close any open Office applications so the add-in registers cleanly during setup.
2. Launch Excel, Outlook, or Word and look for the Kutools and Kutools Plus ribbon tabs.
3. Scan the grouped utilities and hover over a button to see exactly what it does.
4. Try a quick one-click task, such as removing blank rows or merging a batch of files.
5. Begin your free trial or enter your license when asked to unlock the complete toolset.

## ❓ Common Questions
**Is Kutools free?**
Kutools is a paid product that comes with a free trial for evaluation. You can explore every utility during the trial period; there is no free full version and no workaround involved — you simply download the trial.

**What is Kutools for Excel?**
Kutools for Excel is the most popular product in the suite — an add-in that installs hundreds of extra features into Excel, from batch operations and workbook merging to advanced find and one-click cleanup, all on a dedicated ribbon tab.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
Kutools for Windows works on current 64-bit versions of Windows such as Windows 10 and Windows 11, together with a supported install of Microsoft Office or Microsoft 365.

**Is my data secure?**
Kutools operates locally as an add-in inside your own Office apps, so your documents and mailboxes never leave your machine. See the material bundled with the app for full detail on data handling.
